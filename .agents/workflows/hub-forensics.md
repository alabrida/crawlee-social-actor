# Workflow — Business Hub Forensics

> **Objective:** Perform forensic inspection of the business website to detect technical and marketing maturity.

---

## 1. Phase: 🔬 INSPECT — Technical Signals
**Role:** Hub-Forensics Agent
1. Verify SSL Certificate.
2. Search HTML for script patterns:
   - Google Analytics (G-, UA-)
   - Facebook Pixel
   - HubSpot Tracking
3. Check for JSON-LD schema objects.

## 2. Phase: ⚖️ COMPLIANCE — Privacy & Consent
**Role:** Hub-Forensics Agent
1. Detect Cookie Consent banners (selectors or keywords).
2. Find Privacy Policy and Terms of Service links.

## 3. Phase: 🎯 CONVERSION — Asset Detection
**Role:** Hub-Forensics Agent
1. Identify Newsletter signup forms.
2. Locate high-value CTAs (Book Now, Free Trial).
3. Find Lead Magnets (PDF links, Guide keywords).

## 4. Phase: 🟢 RECIPROCITY — Social Check
**Role:** Link-Strategist Agent
1. Scan footer for social icons.
2. Verify if icons link to the same profiles currently being scraped.
