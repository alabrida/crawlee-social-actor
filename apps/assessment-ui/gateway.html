Account Connection Gateway

To retrieve social insights and perform scoring, the diagnostic engine requires active session cookies for LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter/X. Choose how you would like to connect your accounts.

Browser Extension Sync

Sync credentials directly from your currently active browser tabs. Extracts session tokens locally, keeping your master passwords fully private. Bypasses multi-factor auth (MFA) challenges automatically.

  • ✓ One-Click Sync: Batch import all active platform sessions
  • ✓ No Password Sharing: Only session cookies leave your browser
  • ✓ Zero Setup Complexity: Connects instantly in the background
