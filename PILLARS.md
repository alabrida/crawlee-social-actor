# PILLARS.md: Apify (Brand & Positioning)

## 1. Project Mission & Objectives
- **Mission**: High-velocity data extraction and signal detection microservice.
- **Goal**: Automate the collection of market signals, competitor data, and lead indicators to feed the Revenue Engine.
- **Revenue Journey Alignment**: Awareness (Signal Detection) and Consideration (Market Intelligence).

## 2. Core Pillars (Revenue Architecture)

### Trust
- [ ] **Data Provenance**: Every extracted record must include a source URL and timestamp.
- [ ] **Privacy First**: Strictly adhere to platform robots.txt and ethical scraping standards.

### Transparency
- [ ] **Extraction Logs**: Maintain detailed logs of all actor runs and credit consumption.
- [ ] **Schema Clarity**: Use standardized JSON schemas for all output datasets.

### Results
- [ ] **Information Velocity**: Automate the transfer of signals to the Librarian (Agent 3) Knowledge Base.
- [ ] **Lead Enrichment**: Use scraping to automatically populate missing indicators in the Consulting Funnel.
